PLACEHOLDER FILES - Replace with your PNG designs

Place your cat PNG files here:

1. head.png    - Cat head (faces right →)
2. body.png    - Cat body segment (repeatable)  
3. tail.png    - Cat tail/back legs (faces left ←)

All files should be:
- PNG format with transparency
- Same dimensions (recommended: 40x40 or 80x80 pixels)
- Transparent background
- High quality for crisp scaling

Once you add the PNG files, delete this PLACEHOLDER.txt file.

The game will automatically detect and use your PNG assets!
